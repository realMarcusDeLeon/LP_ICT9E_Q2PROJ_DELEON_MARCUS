Pizza

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Total Time: 25 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

1. Flour 2. Water 3. Yeast 4. Salt 5. Olive oil 6. Sugar 7. Tomatoes 8. Garlic 9. Basil 10. Oregano 11. Mozzarella 12. Parmesan 13. Pepperoni 14. Sausage 15. Ham 16. Bacon 17. Mushrooms 18. Bell peppers 19. Onions 20. Olives 21. Spinach 22. Pineapple 23. Anchovies 24. Red pepper flakes

Directions

    1. Prepare Dough: Mix flour, water, yeast, salt, olive oil, and sugar. Knead until smooth, then let rise for 1-2 hours. 2. Make Sauce: Sauté garlic in olive oil, add tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes. 3. Preheat Oven: Heat to 475°F (245°C). 4. Assemble Pizza: Roll out dough, spread sauce, add cheese, and top with your choice of toppings. 5. Bake: Place pizza on a baking sheet or stone, bake for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly. 6. Serve: Remove from oven, slice, and enjoy!
