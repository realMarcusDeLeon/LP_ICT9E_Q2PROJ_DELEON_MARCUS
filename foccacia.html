Foccacia Col Formaggio

Ingredients

  • Bread Flour
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Cheese

Directions

  1. Mix together the dough ingredients in a medium mixing bowl with a spoon. Turn the dough out and knead it until you have a smooth ball. Feel free to use a stand-mixer for this to make it a little quicker. Let it rest before rolling.
  2. Cut the rested dough into 4 pieces and roll the first one out to a very large circle until paper thin. You can use your hands to stretch it a little, hold it up and let the weight of it stretch it out and roll. Once it’s thin enough to see your hand through, it’s good to go. At this point it should be about 14-15 inches wide.
  3. Lay the first sheet of dough over a 12 inch pizza tray so that it’s hanging over all the edges. Now tear off pieces of cheese and scatter them all over the dough sheet. Top it with another sheet of rolled out dough. Now trim off the overhang, then roll and press the edges of the dough together all the way round.
  4. Brush the top with olive oil, sprinkle over salt then tear 4-5 little holes all over to let steam escape. Bake in a very hot oven for 12-15 minutes.
